About Us

IMPACTLUTION provides cost-effective, tronsformative, and sustainable CSR event solutions, that are deep rooted in the values of the Sustainable Development Goals. We are focused on sustainable management methodology and framework that includes Impact Reporting as a communication strategy to convey the change created by an organisation or activity, communicating and learning from measurable data which is critical to keep track of the impact delivered.

We organise projects, workshops, and trainings that are designed towards sustainable community development, waste management, and education, and awareness in the field of environment and sustainability practices.

The Organisation

Our Services

CSR Event Management

Our CSR events are crafted to ensure awareness and education to encourage mindset and behavior change. We believe that with this ownership in being the change agent, we are able to see real and lasting change in both the "giver" and the "receiver".

Our programs are research-based, with site visitation. Impact reporting that comes with sustainability and feasibility test is central to the programs.

Team Building

We have a unique team-building solution for your staff that would engage them in purposeful volunteering projects with a positive social and environmental outcome.

We aspire to promote and evolve the concept of teambuilding, moving beyond staff motivation and morale to transformative teambuilding, which is based on the principles of creativity, scalability, responsiveness and corporate shared values.
